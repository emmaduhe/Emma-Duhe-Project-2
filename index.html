About Us!

Lucky Court is a place for pickleball lovers to get together with the community to play their favorite game and enjoy a ton of other amenities! We are home to not only 8 courts, but to local food trucks, unique bars, free pickleball lessons, comfortable lounges, and an arcade for kids and kids at heart! Whether you are stopping by for a quick match or spending the entire day with friends and family, Lucky Court offers an exciting and welcoming atmosphere where competition, connection, and entertainment all come together in one vibrant space.
